About The Shift Chiropractic

The Shift Chiropractic was founded on a simple belief: that everyone deserves access to quality, immediate chiropractic care when they need it most. Our practice began in Oakland with a mission to disrupt the traditional healthcare model where patients wait weeks for appointments and months for relief. We recognized that pain doesn't follow a schedule, and neither should access to professional chiropractic treatment. Today, we've grown into Oakland's most trusted same-day chiropractic clinic, serving thousands of patients who appreciate our commitment to immediate care and exceptional results.

Our core values center on patient-first care, clinical excellence, and community wellness. Every member of our team is dedicated to understanding your unique condition and creating a personalized treatment plan that delivers real results. We believe in combining evidence-based chiropractic techniques with compassionate patient care, ensuring that every visitor to our clinic leaves feeling heard, treated, and on the path to recovery. We're not just treating pain—we're transforming lives and helping our Oakland community live healthier, more mobile, and more fulfilling lives.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers. Here are the most common questions we receive.

We welcome both walk-ins and scheduled appointments! You can absolutely walk in whenever you're experiencing pain or discomfort, and we'll fit you in as quickly as possible. However, if you prefer to schedule ahead, you can book online through our Acuity Scheduling system or call us directly. Walk-ins are typically seen within 10-15 minutes during regular business hours, depending on how busy we are that day.

Many patients report feeling relief immediately after their first adjustment, though the degree varies depending on your condition. Some experience significant pain reduction within the first 24-48 hours, while others notice gradual improvement over the first week. Most patients see substantial results within 3-5 sessions. The key is consistency with your treatment plan—we'll work with you to create a customized schedule that maximizes your recovery and prevents future issues.

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. We recommend checking your specific policy to understand your coverage, deductibles, and any referral requirements. Our front desk team can help verify your insurance benefits before your first visit. We also offer competitive cash rates for those without insurance or who prefer to pay out-of-pocket. Contact us at theshiftchiropractic@gmail.com to discuss your insurance coverage and payment options.

We treat a wide range of conditions including back pain, neck pain, headaches, sciatica, sports injuries, whiplash, carpal tunnel syndrome, and general musculoskeletal issues. Our chiropractors are experienced in both acute injuries and chronic pain management. During your first visit, we'll conduct a thorough examination and discuss your symptoms to determine the best treatment approach for your specific condition. If we determine that your condition requires specialized care beyond our scope, we'll provide appropriate referrals.

Your first visit typically takes 30-45 minutes. We'll start with a comprehensive consultation about your medical history, current symptoms, and health goals. Next, our chiropractor will perform a thorough physical examination and may take X-rays if necessary. Based on our findings, we'll discuss your diagnosis and create a personalized treatment plan tailored to your needs. If appropriate, we may perform your first adjustment during this visit. We'll explain everything in detail and answer all your questions to ensure you feel comfortable and informed.
